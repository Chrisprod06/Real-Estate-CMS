User Manual for Manualy Inserted Interested people

In this screen you can enter a customer who does not have an account, in case there is no property available on the website you are interested in. The purpose of this function is to inform the customer in case you have an available property on the website. a property in the future that suits the client's needs.

  1. Add to the list of interested parties:

    First click on the "Add to Interested List" button if you want to make a new entry. Customer.

  2. Fields Description:

    • Name (required): here you can enter the name of the person concerned.

    • Surname (required): here you can enter the surname of the person concerned.

    • Telephone (required): here you can enter the telephone number of the person concerned.

    • Email: here you can enter the email of the person.

    • Subject (required): here you can enter the subject that concerns the person concerned.

    • Message: Here you can enter more details about the customer and their preferences.

  3. Delete a customer from the manual list of interested parties:

    First click on the cart icon which is located in the last column in the stakeholders table and a window will open asking you if you want to delete the stakeholder. Then click delete and a window will open asking you if you want to ask if you want to delete the request. If you press yes then the customer's entry will be deleted from the system for that customer.

  4. Generate report:

    When you click on the Create Report button, a report will be created with the customer details and messages in PDF format.
