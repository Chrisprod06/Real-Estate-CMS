Communication List User Manual

On this screen you can view information and messages for customers who are messaging for general information.

  1. Delete a customer from the Contact List:

    First click on the cart icon which is located in the last column in the contact panel and a window will open asking you if you want to delete the corresponding entry. Then click delete and a window will appear asking if you want to ask for the corresponding question. the customer's entry will be deleted from the system.

  2. Create report:

    When you click on the Create Report button, a report will be created with the customer details and messages in the format pdf.
